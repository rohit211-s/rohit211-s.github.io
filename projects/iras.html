GroundWater Forecasting:

Univariate and Multivariate Analysis With N-BEATS

  • Github URL: Project Link

This project focuses on forecasting groundwater levels through two distinct approaches: univariate and multivariate data analysis. In the univariate analysis, we leverage past observations to predict the current day's groundwater level using three trained models: N-Beats, LSTM, and Conv1d Layer. The N-Beats model is specifically designed for time series data, while LSTM excels at capturing long-term dependencies in sequential data, and Conv1d Layer detects local patterns. In the multivariate analysis, we extend the approach by incorporating additional environmental features, such as precipitation, temperature, and atmospheric vapor pressure, which may influence groundwater dynamics. This integration allows us to develop more accurate predictive models, enabling a deeper understanding of the complex relationships between these features and their combined impact on groundwater levels.
