Enhancing Student Placement Predictions with EQ and IQ Analysis


As a dedicated Data Science Developer, I undertook the project of 'Enhancing Student Placement Predictions with EQ and IQ Analysis' with the goal of forecasting students' placement performance through a comprehensive assessment of both Emotional Quotient (EQ) and Intellectual Quotient (IQ). In this endeavor, I utilized a real-time dataset and employed various cutting-edge machine learning, deep learning, and hybrid models to derive accurate predictions. Leveraging my expertise in TensorFlow, OpenCV, Python, Matplotlib, and Scikit-Learn, I meticulously developed and trained the models to optimize their performance. Employing sophisticated techniques such as Boosting and Bagging, I further enhanced the accuracy of the predictions. The insights gained from this research not only provide valuable guidance to educators and institutions but also highlight the significance of a holistic evaluation of EQ and IQ in maximizing students' career prospects.
